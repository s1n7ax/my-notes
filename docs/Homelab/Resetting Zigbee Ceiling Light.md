# Resetting Zigbee Ceiling Light

Sequence to put the Zigbee ceiling light into pairing mode:

1. Turn **off** and keep it off for **5 seconds**
2. Turn **on**
3. Turn **off** after **1 second**
4. Wait **3 seconds**
5. Turn **on**
6. Turn **off** after **1 second**
7. Wait **3 seconds**
8. Turn **on** again, then stop

After a few seconds the light will start **blinking**, indicating it is in pairing mode.
